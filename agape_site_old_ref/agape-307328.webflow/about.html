ägapē

Born in the heart of Brooklyn, Ägape has grown into a cornerstone of the underground techno scene, locally and internationally. Since day one, the transformative power of music has driven the mission, inspiring immersive experiences that transcend borders and forge From raw, intimate warehouse gatherings to full-scale, high-impact events, Ägape delivers uncompromising production, boundary-pushing sound design, and truly inclusive energy. This is Ägape. Welcome to the movement.

Ägape was born in the shadows of New York City’s warehouse scene—fueled by a love for raw, uncompromising techno and the desire to create something that didn’t yet exist. What started as a DIY passion project quickly evolved into a trusted name in underground music, known for curating nights that are as intentional as they are intense. Founded by artists and ravers who understood the power of sound to unite, Agapē emerged not just to throw parties, but to build culture. With roots planted firmly in Brooklyn, we’ve stayed true to our ethos: high-impact music, no fluff, and a spotlight on both global innovators and the local scene’s freshest talent.From secret locations to world-class venues, Agapē continues to grow—but always carries the spirit of its origin: raw energy, deep connection, and full dedication to the underground.

Origins & Evolution

Ägape transcends the conventional boundaries of a music collective. Rooted in Brooklyn's vibrant underground, we've evolved into a collective global force, curating events that resonate with the pulse of hard techno enthusiasts worldwide. Our journey spans from intimate warehouse gatherings to international stages, consistently delivering high-impact experiences that celebrate the raw, At the heart of our mission lies a commitment to championing New York's local underground scene. We provide a platform for emerging talent, fostering a community where innovation thrives and boundaries are pushed. Through our events, we aim to forge genuine connections, uniting artists and audiences in a shared passion for transformative soundscapes.This is Ägape, a movement where raw energy meets global ambition, and the underground finds its voice.
